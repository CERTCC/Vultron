---
title: DR-05 Accept.object_ must carry the original Invite
type: learning
date: 2026-04-20
source: REVIEW-26042001-DR-05
---

`InviteReceivedUseCase` MUST stash the received invite activity on the BT
blackboard. `AcceptInviteNode` MUST read the invite from the blackboard and
use it as `object_` when constructing the `Accept` activity. Passing the case
object or embargo event as `object_` produces coercion failures.

**Promoted**: 2026-04-28 — captured in `notes/activitystreams-semantics.md`;
pitfall in `AGENTS.md`.
