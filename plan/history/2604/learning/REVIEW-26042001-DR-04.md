---
title: DR-04 Fail-fast for required event fields
type: learning
date: 2026-04-20
source: REVIEW-26042001-DR-04
---

Required fields in `*ReceivedEvent` classes MUST be typed as non-optional.
Pydantic validation MUST raise at construction time. A use case MUST NOT raise
`ValueError` inside `execute()` for missing required fields.
