title: DR-08 AttachNoteToCaseNode idempotency
type: learning
date: 2026-04-20
source: REVIEW-26042001-DR-08

`AttachNoteToCaseNode.update()` MUST check `note.id_ in case.notes` for
idempotency, NOT `dl.read(note_id) is not None`. A note may exist in the
DataLayer without being attached to the case.

**Promoted**: 2026-04-28 — captured in `notes/bt-integration.md`;
requirement in `specs/case-management.yaml` CM-13-007.
