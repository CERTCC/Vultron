title: DR-06 Multi-party embargo per-participant EM state
type: learning
date: 2026-04-20
source: REVIEW-26042001-DR-06

Each `CaseParticipant` carries its own EM state. Case EM state transitions to
`ACTIVE` when the case owner accepts. Non-owner participants mutate only their
own consent state. `case.attributed_to == actor_id` identifies the case owner.

**Promoted**: 2026-04-28 — captured in `notes/participant-embargo-consent.md`;
formal requirements in `specs/case-management.yaml` CM-13.
