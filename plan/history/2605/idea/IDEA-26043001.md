## IDEA-26043001 append-history should reject future dates

The `append-history` CLI tool accepts any date without checking
whether that date is in the future. This means agents can accidentally
create history entries dated weeks or months ahead without any warning.

**Processed**: 2026-05-01 — combined with IDEA-26043002 and IDEA-26043003
into `specs/history-management.yaml` HM-06 and HM-07 (v1.2.0).
Future-date rejection (60-second tolerance) implemented in CLI write path;
see `notes/history-management.md`.
