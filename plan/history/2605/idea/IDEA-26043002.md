## IDEA-26043002 append-history should use tz-aware timestamps

History entry frontmatter uses bare `date: YYYY-MM-DD` which discards
sub-day ordering information and is timezone-ambiguous. High-throughput
sessions can produce multiple entries per day in undefined order.

**Processed**: 2026-05-01 — combined with IDEA-26043001 and IDEA-26043003
into `specs/history-management.yaml` HM-06 and HM-07 (v1.2.0).
`date:` field replaced by `timestamp: datetime (UTC ISO 8601)` with
automatic legacy conversion; model migrated; 57 existing entries migrated.
