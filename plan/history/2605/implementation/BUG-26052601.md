Symptom: VultronActivityConstructionError in all exchange demo tests (24 failures).

Root cause: rm_validate_report_activity, rm_invalidate_report_activity, and
rm_close_report_activity used cast(RmSubmitReportActivity, offer) to satisfy
the type-checker. cast() is a no-op at runtime; Pydantic v2 rejects the plain
as_Offer when it tries to assign it to the typed object_ field.

Fix: Added isinstance guard + RmSubmitReportActivity.model_validate()
coercion before construction in each of the three factory functions.
Also added public parse_submit_report_offer() factory helper.
